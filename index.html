Methodology

From an Idea
to Ink on Paper

This all started as a simple idea: how do we visualize a life? Today, it involves researching our favorite icons, translating their life events into code, and using a Bantam Tools NextDraw™ 2234 large format plotter to physically draw their timelines using archival ink.

        y = noise.pnoise1(i + 100)

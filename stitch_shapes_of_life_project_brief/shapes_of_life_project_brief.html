"Shapes of Life" Pen Plotter Art Project Project Overview: A Python-driven art project that visualizes the lives of famous icons through hash marks and significant events. - Data: Birth date, death date, and key life events. - Visualization: Weekly hash marks (6 vertical, 1 horizontal) grouped by year. - Production: 19 x 24 inch canvas, plotted on a pen plotter with archival pens. - Scale: Up to 29,000+ hash marks per piece; 8-hour plot time. - Current Subjects: David Bowie, Johnny Cash. - Planned Subjects: Prince, Kurt Cobain, Jimi Hendrix. - Sales Model: Limited edition releases (e.g., 50 per artist). Design Goals for Website: 1. Showcase the intricate detail of the plots. 2. Tell the technical and artistic story of the process. 3. Create a high-end, gallery-like e-commerce experience. 4. Highlight the "Limited Edition" nature of the physical prints.

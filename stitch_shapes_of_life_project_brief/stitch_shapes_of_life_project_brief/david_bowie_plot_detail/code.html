The Narrative Record

This piece is a chronometric mapping of David Bowie's creative output. Each of the 29,412 hash marks corresponds to a specific timeframe within his active years.

The data is grouped by week and year, creating a density map that reveals the intense periods of transformation and the quiet lulls of preparation. The plot took over 8 hours to complete, a meditative mechanical translation of a life lived at the edge of the avant-garde.
